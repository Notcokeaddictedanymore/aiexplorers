💡 关键发现Key insight： 同样的像素，看得越清楚你越能认出。但 AI 不会"恍然大悟"——它只是把每个像素的颜色数字相加、对比，找规律。它不需要"看清楚"也能识别，因为它处理的不是"图像"，是数字。 Clearer = easier for you. But AI never has an "aha moment" — it just adds & compares color numbers. AI doesn't need clarity because it processes numbers, not "images."

Your ticks become 0s and 1s for AI. That's literally how AI "sees" the image:

这就叫特征向量（Feature Vector）。所有现代图像 AI，看完图后都会得到这种"长串数字"，再用它来分类。

That's a feature vector. Every modern image AI gets one of these after looking — then uses it to classify.

💡 关键洞察Key insight

同样的图片，可以被打成无数种不同标签。标签不是事实，是选择——你（或者公司、政府）选了哪种标签方式，AI 学到的世界就长那个样子。

The same image can wear countless labels. A label is not a fact, it's a choice. Whoever picks the labels (you, a company, a government) shapes the world AI learns.

"可爱程度"标签没有客观答案——但如果 AI 学了它，就会把"不可爱"的动物打低分。这就是偏差的源头。

"Cuteness" has no objective answer — but if AI learns it, it'll downrank "uncute" animals. This is where bias begins.

AI 大概率会说"不是狗"。因为它的"狗特征"被偏差缩窄了：金黄色 ✓、长毛 ✓、垂耳 ✓。哈士奇全不符合。AI不会反思"这可能是另一种狗"，它只会查相似度。

AI most likely says "not a dog". Its "dog features" got narrowed by bias: golden color ✓, long fur ✓, floppy ears ✓. Husky fails all 3. AI doesn't reason "maybe it's another breed" — it just checks similarity.

这不是 AI 的错——是给它例子的人的错。这也是为什么"训练数据多样性"在 M1-3 那么重要。

This is not AI's fault. It's the trainer's. That's why "training-data diversity" matters so much in M1-3.

📚 真实事件True story： 谷歌的早期图像 AI 把肤色较深的人脸误识为"黑猩猩"。原因之一就是训练数据里深肤色样本太少。事件让谷歌道歉、关闭了部分功能。 Google's early image AI labeled darker-skinned faces as "gorillas". Cause: underrepresented in training data. Google had to apologize and disable features.
