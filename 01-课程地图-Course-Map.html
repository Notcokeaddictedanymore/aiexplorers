认识智小灵 / Lingxi Meet Lingxi

一只蓝色全息小灵兽，会变形成放大镜、计算器、地球仪。它不卖萌、不说教，用苏格拉底式反问引导你自己想清楚。它会承认"我也不知道"，甚至偶尔露出 AI 的幻觉——让你学会质疑 AI。 A blue holographic spirit-creature that morphs into magnifiers, calculators, globes. No cuteness, no preaching — Socratic questions only. It admits "I don't know" and even hallucinates on purpose so you learn to question AI.

导师对白样本
Sample dialogue
"你刚才让我识别这张图，我说'是猫'，可它明明是只狗。我是不是哪里没学好？"
"You asked me what's in the picture and I said 'cat' — but it's clearly a dog. Did I learn something wrong?"
