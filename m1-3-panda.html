💡 关键洞察Key insight

AI 看到的不是"小朋友"或"熊猫"这两个概念。它只看到：黑白配色 ✓、眼睛周围的黑色块 ✓、像熊猫的形状 ✓——所以它说"是熊猫"。AI 不懂"概念"，它只算"特征像不像"。这就是为什么 AI 会被表象骗。

AI doesn't see "kid" or "panda" as concepts. It sees: black-white ✓, dark eye patches ✓, panda-like shape ✓ — so it guesses "panda." AI doesn't grasp concepts, only feature similarity. That's why surface looks can fool it.

🧠 AI 小百科🧠 AI Mini-Dictionary

Training Data
训练数据Training Data

AI 学习用的"课本"。课本上的例子越多样，AI 学得越好——不只是数量多。

AI's "textbook." Variety matters more than quantity.

Label
标签Label

你告诉 AI "这张是熊猫"——"熊猫"就是标签。你刚才点的绿/红圈都是在打标签。

When you tell AI "this is a panda," "panda" is the label. The green/red dots you clicked were labels.

Classification
分类Classification

AI 把东西归到不同的"盒子"里。今天你做的是"熊猫 / 不是熊猫"两类。也可以分 1000 类（如 ImageNet）。

AI sorting things into "boxes." Today: panda vs not-panda (2 boxes). Could be 1,000 (e.g. ImageNet).

Bias ⚠️
偏差Bias

如果你只给 AI 看穿白衣服的人，AI 会以为"人都穿白衣服"。你的训练数据"不公平"，AI 学到的也"不公平"。

If AI only sees people in white, it thinks everyone wears white. Unfair training data → unfair AI.
