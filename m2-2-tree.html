决策树是 AI 最早的"思考方式"之一。和我们 M1-3 用的神经网络比一比——它们各有所长。

Decision trees are one of AI's oldest reasoning styles. Compared to the neural network we used in M1-3, each shines differently.

🌳 决策树Decision Tree
白盒 · 看得见怎么想的White-box · transparent
  • ✅ 可解释：每个判断都能"指给你看"——非常适合医疗、法律、金融Explainable: every decision can be shown — perfect for medicine/law/finance
  • ✅ 不需要大量数据：几条规则就能开始用Low data hunger: a handful of rules works
  • ❌ 很难处理图像/声音：因为这些数据有上百万维特征Bad at images/sound: too many feature dimensions
  • ❌ 遇到新情况会卡住：树没分到的就不会答Brittle on novelty: any input not in the tree breaks it
🧠 神经网络Neural Network
黑盒 · 它自己也"说不清"Black-box · it "just feels right"
  • ✅ 擅长高维数据：图像、声音、视频，神经网络无敌Crushes high-dim data: images, audio, video
  • ✅ 泛化能力强：见过几张猫的照片，能认出从没见过的猫Generalizes: learns abstract patterns
  • ❌ 难解释：医生听 AI 说"是癌症"，没法问"为什么"Hard to explain: "why?" gets a shrug
  • ❌ 很吃数据：动辄需要几千几万个例子才能训好Data hungry: needs thousands of examples
💡 关键洞察Key insight ：现代 AI 经常组合两者——神经网络看图像、决策树做最后的可解释判断。比如医院的 AI 先用神经网络分析 CT，再用决策树告诉医生"为什么"。 : Modern AI often combines both — neural nets do perception, decision trees do final explainable judgments. Hospitals run NN on CT scans, then a tree explains the "why" to doctors.

决策树看起来这么好——可解释、透明、稳。但它也有致命弱点。来一个真实情境，你来判断：

Trees look great — explainable, transparent, sturdy. But they have a fatal weakness. Real scenario, your call:

⚠️ 案例：玉器市场出现一种新材料Case: a new jade-like material appears on the market

某商家把一种新合成材料做得：重量像玉、半透明像玉、雕工精细、表面有人工"包浆"。林师傅那棵 4 个问题的决策树——会怎么判？A vendor synthesizes new material that's: jade-weight, jade-translucent, intricately carved, artificially "patinated". What will Master Lin's 4-question tree say?

📌 真实答案：AReal answer: A

树会非常自信地说"御用玉"——因为它走完了 4 个 Yes 的路径，根本不会"怀疑"。这就是决策树的盲区：规则之外的世界，它认不出。

The tree confidently says "royal jade" — because all 4 yeses path there. It never "doubts." That's the blind spot: outside its rules, the tree can't see.

这也是为什么真正的鉴定师会:1）多问"我有没有见过类似的"；2）借助显微镜、化学测试；3）和其他师傅讨论——这些都是树装不进去的能力。

That's why real appraisers 1) ask "have I seen this before?"; 2) use microscopes & chemistry; 3) consult colleagues — abilities trees can't represent.

📚 真实事件True story： 2010 年代翡翠造假技术（合成、染色、注胶）大爆发，许多用决策树的早期鉴定 AI 集体失灵，被骗子用来背书假货。后来行业引入光谱分析+神经网络组合，才扛住。 In the 2010s, jade-faking tech (synthesis, dyeing, gel injection) exploded. Many early decision-tree AIs failed and were used to "certify" fakes. Industry shifted to spectroscopy + NN hybrids.
