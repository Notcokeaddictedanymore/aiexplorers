AI 出错有两种不一样的方式。先学这两个词，再用它们看世界：

AI errors come in two flavors. Learn both, then use them to read the world:

⚠️

假阳性（False Positive）False Positive

AI 喊"狼来了"——其实没有。
例子：把无辜路人识别成通缉犯。

AI cries wolf — but there's no wolf.
Example: tagging innocent passersby as wanted criminals.

🚨

假阴性（False Negative）False Negative

狼来了——AI 没看见。
例子：CT 里有早期肿瘤，AI 漏诊。

Wolf is real — AI missed it.
Example: an early tumor in a CT scan that AI overlooks.
