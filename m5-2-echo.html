Last mission: each tap teaches AI about you. Today's key question: once AI really "knows" you, what does your world look like?

🔬 今天我让你做实验：你扮演两个完全不同的人，分别刷我的模拟视频流。最后对比——同一个 APP 给两个人造出了什么样的两个世界。

🔬 Today's experiment: play two very different people, each scrolls my simulated feed. Then compare — same app, what two worlds did it build?

这种现象有个名字——信息茧房（Filter Bubble / Echo Chamber）。哥伦比亚大学 2021 年研究：同样话题，给两个用户看，2 周后他们看到的已经是两个互相不知道对方在看什么的世界。

This phenomenon has a name — filter bubble / echo chamber. A 2021 Columbia study: 2 users, same topic, 2 weeks later — they\'re in two worlds neither knows the other inhabits.
