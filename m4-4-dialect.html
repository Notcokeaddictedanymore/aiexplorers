中国有 80+ 种方言和少数民族语言。普通话是其中一种。但 ChatGPT/文心/豆包 这些 AI 受过训练的大部分中文数据，是普通话。

China has 80+ dialects and minority languages. Mandarin is one. But ChatGPT/Wenxin/Doubao were trained on mostly Mandarin Chinese data.

后果：如果你只跟 AI 说普通话，AI 越来越懂你。如果你说上海话/客家话/晋语……AI 越来越听不懂。10 年后，你的方言可能没人会说了。

Consequence: speak Mandarin → AI knows you better. Speak Shanghainese/Hakka/Jin… AI falls behind. In 10 years, your dialect could be gone.
