🦋 蝴蝶效应🦋 The Butterfly Effect

1972 年气象学家 Lorenz 发现：巴西丛林一只蝴蝶扇动翅膀，3 周后可能引起德州的龙卷风。这听起来荒谬，但数学上是真的。

1972 meteorologist Lorenz found: a butterfly flapping in Brazil may trigger a tornado in Texas 3 weeks later. Sounds absurd — mathematically true.

💡 对 AI 意味着什么？What it means for AI
  • AI 越往远预测越不准。6 小时预测 90% 准；3 天预测 60% 准；2 周预测 = 蒙
  • AI gets worse the further out. 6hr: 90% accurate; 3 days: 60%; 2 weeks: guessing
  • AI 不会给"100% 会下暴雨"——它会给"85% 概率"。这就是为什么气象预报都说"降水概率"
  • AI doesn\'t say "will rain 100%" — it says "85% chance". That\'s why forecasts use "precipitation probability"
  • 多个 AI 模型同时跑，结果不同时，气象员要人工判断——AI 不能完全替代人
  • Multiple AI models run in parallel; when they disagree, humans judge — AI can\'t fully replace experts

🔗 它们怎么协作How they cooperate： 国家气象中心说"北京未来 6 小时降雨 100mm" → 北京市排水 AI 说"具体莲花桥下穿、广渠门桥会积水超 1m" → 防汛办决定派车去这些点。没有这条链条，宏观警报到不了具体位置。 National Met says "Beijing 100mm in 6hr" → Beijing drainage AI says "specifically Lianhua Bridge underpass, Guangqumen will flood above 1m" → emergency office dispatches there. Without this chain, macro warnings can\'t reach specific spots.
