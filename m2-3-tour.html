📌 真实答案：CReal answer: C

"我应该先看哪件"没有客观答案。它依赖：

"Which should I see first?" has no objective answer. It depends on:

  • 你的兴趣——历史？艺术？工艺？your interest — history? art? craft?
  • 你的时间——10 分钟还是 3 小时？your time — 10 min or 3 hours?
  • 你的体力——和小朋友/老人一起？your stamina — kids/elders with you?
  • 当时的人流——哪个展厅在排队？crowd levels — which gallery is packed?
  • 你的情绪——今天想看震撼的还是温柔的？your mood — bold or gentle vibes today?

这些都是主观、情境、个人化的信息。它们没法写进知识图谱——因为没有固定答案。

These are subjective, contextual, personal. They can't be stored as facts — they have no fixed answer.

📚 现代 AI 怎么应对？How modern AI handles this
真实的 AI 导游会反问："您今天有多少时间？最感兴趣的是？"——用对话挖掘个人偏好，然后给推荐。但这个推荐仍然是猜的。 Real AI guides ask back: "How much time? What interests you most?" — using dialogue to mine preference, then guess. The recommendation is still a guess.

这一关你学到了：AI 不是无所不知——它的"知道"取决于谁给了它什么知识，以及问题本身是否有客观答案。

You learned: AI isn't omniscient — its "knowing" depends on what facts it's given AND whether the question has an objective answer at all.
